Analyze

Click Analyze after create the shading scene to generate shading data. You can then export shading data for use in simulation models and other software. This section is a quick reference for the Analyze options. For more details see:

•Generate shade data
•Export shade data

Diurnal Analysis

Click this button to generate one or more tables of hour-by-month shade fractions or beam shading factors.

When your scene contains more than one active surface, you can generate a separate hour-by-month table for the surfaces by assigning group names to the surfaces. See Generate shading data for details.

Hourly Analysis (entire array)

Click this button to generate a CSV file of shade fraction and beam shading factor values for each of the 8,760 hours in a year.

Shade Fraction

Choose this option to generate tables of shade fractions when you click Diurnal analysis. The shade fraction is the ratio of an active surface's shaded area to its total area.

Beam Shading Factor

Choose this option ot generate tables of beam shading factors when you click Diurnal analysis. The beam shading factor is one minus the shade fraction.
