Savings and Revenue

SAM reports Revenue results for PPA projects (utility financing options), and Savings results for retail projects (residential and commercial financing options).

For PPA projects, the revenue depends on the electricity sales price and the quantity of energy generated by the system. SAM assumes a fixed electricity sales price (PPA price) with a set of optional TOD factors on Time of Delivery Factors page, or on the Thermal Energy Storage page for Concentrating Solar Power projects.

For retail projects, savings depends on the electric rate structure from the Utility Rates page, the quantity of electricity generated by the system, and the electricity usage from the Electric Load page.
