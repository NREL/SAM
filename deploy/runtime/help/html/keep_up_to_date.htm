Keep SAM Up to Date

The SAM team releases new versions of SAM periodically. To find out if your version of SAM is the latest version, check the SAM website's Download page at https://sam.nrel.gov/content/downloads.

SAM's Welcome page also displays news from the SAM team, including announcements of new versions.

Updating SAM

If a newer version of SAM is available on the SAM website that the version installed on your computer, you can download and install it to update SAM. You can either keep the older version on your computer, or remove it using your operating system's uninstall program.
