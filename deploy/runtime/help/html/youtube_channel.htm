Videos

For video demonstrations of SAM's performance and financial models and other features, see the SAM YouTube Channel.

To register for upcoming webinars and round tables, see the Webinars page of the SAM website. You can also find links to recordings of past webinars on that page. A round table is a 30-minute online session with the SAM team where you can ask questions and provide feedback.

Note. The videos may show features in older versions of SAM that have been updated since the recording was made.
