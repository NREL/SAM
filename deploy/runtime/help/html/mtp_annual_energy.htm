Annual Energy

SAM reports the total quantity of electricity delivered by the system in Year one of the project cash flow and reports it in the Metrics table:

•For front-of-meter projects (PPA, Merchant Plant, Community Solar) financial models, SAM assumes that all of the electricity is sold to the grid.

•For distributed behind-the-meter projects (Residential, Commercial, Third Party Ownership), some of the electricity generated by the system meets a building or facility load that you specify on the Electric Load page, and some earns net metering credits or is sold to the grid at the retail rates you specify on the Electricity Rates page.

Annual Energy, kWh/year

All performance models except solar water heating systems.

Annual Energy = Sum of Hourly Energy Delivered

Annual Energy Saved, kWh/year

For solar water heating systems, the annual energy saved is the electrical energy the project avoids purchasing due to the solar water heating system. SeeAux with and without Solar for details.
