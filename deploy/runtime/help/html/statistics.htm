Statistics

The Statistics tab displays statistical summaries of the time series data. You can show mean, minimum, maximum, total, standard deviation, average daily minimum, and average daily maximum over the entire year or by month.

To export data from the table, right-click it (control click on a mouse with one button) and choose an option.
