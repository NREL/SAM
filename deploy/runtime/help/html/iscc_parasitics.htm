Parasitics

The variables on the Parasitics page define electrical loads in the system. For each hour of the simulation, SAM calculates the parasitic load and subtracts it from the power cycle's gross electrical output to calculate the net electrical output.

Fraction of gross power consumed at all times

A fixed electric load applied to all hours of the simulation, expressed as a fraction of rated gross power at design from the Tower Receiver Cycle page.

Balance of plant parasitic (MWe/MWcap, solar)

A parasitic load that is applied as a function of the solar thermal input to the power cycle.
