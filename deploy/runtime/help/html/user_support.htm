For information about any page in the software, do one of the following:

Press the F1 key in Windows or Command-? in Mac OS.

Click the help button at the top right corner of each input page.

Click Help Contents from the Help menu.

In secondary windows, click the Help button for information about the window.

For additional help, try:

For general information about the model, including a discussion of project costs, references to related publications and a list of frequently asked questions, and other information visit the SAM website: http://sam.nrel.gov/.

For user support, post a question on the SAM forum at https://sam.nrel.gov/forums/support-forum.

To send an email to the SAM team, contact us at sam.support@nrel.gov.
