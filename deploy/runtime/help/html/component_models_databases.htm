Solar and Wind Resource Files

SAM comes with a of weather files for the solar and wind performance models.

The solar resource files are in the SAM CSV format and contain data from:

•National Solar Resource Database (NSRDB): TMY3 (1991-2005) and TMY2 (1961-1990)
•Solar and Wind Energy Resource Assessment Programme (SWERA)
•The ASHRAE International Weather for Energy Calculations Version 1.1 (IWEC)
•Canadian Weather for Energy Calculations (CWEC)

The wind resource files are in the SRW format and contains representative typical weather files for some locations around the United States developed by NREL and AWS TruePower.
