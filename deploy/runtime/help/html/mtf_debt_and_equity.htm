Debt and Equity

The debt and equity metrics show the amount borrowed and initial cash investment amount for financial models with project term debt. The all equity partnership flip and sale leaseback models do not include debt.

Equity

For projects with term debt, equity is the amount of cash invested by the project owner or investor:

Total equity ($) = Initial cost less cash incentives ($) - Debt ($)

Debt

For projects with term debt, debt is the amount borrowed by the project. How SAM calculates the debt amount depends on the financial model.

Residential and Commercial

For the residential and commercial financial models, size of debt depends on the debt percent, and the initial cost less cash incentives:

Debt = Debt percent (%) × initial cost less cash incentives ($)

Where the debt percent is from the Financial Parameters page.

Single Owner and Leveraged Partnership Flip

For the single owner and leveraged partnership flip financial models, on the Financial Parameters page, you can either specify the debt size as a fraction of installed cost, or specify a debt-service coverage ratio.

When you specify the debt percent as an input, SAM assumes constant debt payments, and reports the debt size and the smallest annual debt service coverage ratio from the cash flow:

Debt = Debt percent (%) × initial cost less cash incentives ($)

When you specify the DSCR as an input, SAM calculates annual debt payments sized as appropriate for the project's annual revenue and expenses assuming a constant annual DSCR. SAM calculates the debt funding amount from the cash available for debt service (CAFDS) in the cash flow, and the term debt interest rate r and constant DSCR from the Financial Parameters page:

CAFDSn is the cash available for debt service in Year n:

CAFDS in Year n= EBIDTA in Year n - Sum of Major Equipment Replacement Reserves in Year n

Where EBIDTA and Major Equipment Reserves are from the cash flow:

•Single Owner
•Leveraged Partnership Flip

Debt Percent

For the single owner and leveraged partnership flip financial models, when you specify the DSCR as an input, the debt percent is:

Debt Percent (%) = Debt ($) ÷ [ Debt ($) + Equity ($) ] × 100%

The all equity partnership flip and sale leaseback financial models do not involve debt.

For the residential and commercial models, the debt percent is an input on the Financial Parameters page, so SAM does not report it in the Metrics table.

Note about debt percent and cash incentives. For the single owner and leveraged partnership flip models, the debt percent applies to the initial cost before subtracting cash incentives. For the residential and commercial models, the debt percent applies to the initial cost less cash incentives.
